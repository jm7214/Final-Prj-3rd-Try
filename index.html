Harbor Islands

New York Harbor is home to three small islands; Governors Island, Ellis Island and Liberty Island. Here you will find several prominent landmarks that provide a sneak peak into old New York's rich history.


Each island is unique. The world-famous symbol of freedom and democracy can be found on Liberty Island, A former U.S. Army military base and U.S. Coast Guard station, Governors Island is the oldest European settlement in New York.
